Shailesh Kulkarni

Co-Founder of AltZor

Brings over 25 years of profound experience in engineering, delivery, and technology leadership, having dedicated his career to building high-performance teams, scaling global product engineering functions, and driving complex, large-scale digital transformation initiatives across multiple industries. His journey from hands-on engineering to overseeing multi-million-dollar delivery portfolios has cultivated a deep-seated understanding of the critical intersection where great products, great teams, and great culture successfully converge.

Throughout his tenure with global ISVs and enterprises, he has been responsible for end-to-end product engineering, modernizing platforms, architecting sophisticated distributed systems, mentoring leaders, and enabling teams to execute with clarity, ownership, and unwavering customer-centricity, establishing strong expertise in cloud platforms, microservices, data-driven engineering, and advanced AI-native delivery models. His leadership philosophy is built on the simple, powerful belief that technology scales most effectively when people feel genuinely empowered, leading him to always focus on building environments where teams are encouraged to take ownership, innovate fearlessly, and deliver with purpose.

At AltZor, Shailesh leverages this extensive experience, depth, and vision to establish a next-generation AI-native engineering partner—one that seamlessly blends technical excellence with human-centered leadership. His primary focus is to help customers accelerate their success through intelligent systems, highly scalable architectures, and future-ready digital ecosystems, all while cultivating a vibrant culture where talent is empowered to grow and thrive. If your organization aligns with the value of purposeful technology, strong engineering fundamentals, and a people-first approach, the foundations are set to create something truly remarkable together.

Girish Chandra

Co-Founder of AltZor

He helps global enterprises prepare for the AI era through advanced data engineering, GenAI automation, and modern product engineering. With over 28 years of experience across technology, strategy, and customer leadership, he combines engineering depth with a passion for building high-performance teams that deliver meaningful business impact.

At Altzor, Girish focuses on shaping the company’s AI-first approach—bringing together data readiness, platform engineering, and intelligent automation to help customers accelerate innovation. He works closely with enterprises, ISVs, and mid-market technology companies to modernize products, improve engineering velocity, and build scalable digital capabilities. His leadership is grounded in a simple belief: strong alignment, curiosity, and disciplined execution are what transform good teams into great ones.

Girish is an active voice in the global technology ecosystem. He leads AI-driven thought leadership initiatives, contributing perspectives on product engineering, data platforms, and enterprise AI adoption. Over the years, these engagements have supported multiple recognitions in leadership assessments focused on product engineering, AI, and digital transformation.

Beyond building businesses, Girish is deeply committed to mentorship and community impact. He advises early-stage startup founders on scaling strategies, and he actively supports young artists by connecting emerging musicians to platforms, collaborators, and opportunities. With an engineering degree from Walchand College of Engineering and an MBA from Wright State University, he brings together technical rigor, business strategy, and a people-centric leadership style.

Away from work, Girish is a world percussionist who enjoys blending acoustic and electronic forms, often collaborating with musicians across genres and geographies. Travel, music, and culture fuel his creativity and shape the way he approaches innovation at Altzor.
