Services / .NET Framework & Java EE Enterprise Development

Overview

.NET Framework and Java EE platforms continue to underpin a significant portion of enterprise technology estates. Across industries such as banking, insurance, manufacturing, healthcare, and the public sector, these platforms support long-running systems that are deeply integrated into business workflows.

Altzor provides sustained engineering support for organizations that rely on these ecosystems and require predictable enhancement, disciplined maintenance, and controlled evolution rather than disruptive rewrites.

What We Do

We work across the full lifecycle of .NET and Java EE applications, focusing on stability, performance, and long-term supportability. Our teams understand the operational realities of mature platforms and work within established enterprise standards.
