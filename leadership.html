Shailesh Kulkarni

Co-Founder, Altzor Digital Solutions

Shailesh Kulkarni, Co-Founder of Altzor, brings over 25 years of profound experience in engineering, delivery, and technology leadership. He has dedicated his career to scaling global product engineering functions and driving complex, large-scale digital transformation initiatives across multiple industries.

At Altzor, Shailesh leverages his extensive depth to establish a next-generation AI-native engineering partner. His primary focus is helping customers accelerate success through intelligent systems, highly scalable architectures, and future-ready digital ecosystems.

Shailesh built his expertise in cloud platforms, microservices, and AI-native delivery models while consistently mentoring leaders. His leadership philosophy is built on the belief that technology scales most effectively when people feel genuinely empowered, allowing him to seamlessly blend technical excellence with human-centered leadership.

Girish Chandra

Co-Founder, Altzor Digital Solutions

Girish Chandra, Co-founder of Altzor Digital Solutions, brings over 28 years of experience in technology strategy and product leadership. He helps global enterprises prepare for the AI era by combining deep engineering roots with a passion for building high-performance teams.

At Altzor, Girish drives the company’s AI-first mandate—unifying data readiness, platform engineering, and intelligent automation for enterprises and ISVs. His leadership mantra centers on three pillars: alignment, curiosity, and disciplined execution—the true catalysts for organizational greatness.

Beyond business, Girish is an active thought leader and mentor to early-stage founders. He holds an engineering degree from Walchand College of Engineering and an MBA from Wright State University. Away from work, he is a world percussionist who views music and cross-cultural collaboration as essential fuel for innovation.
